Cheesy Pasta Bake with Chicken and Bacon

Description:

Delicious cheesy pasta filled with hearty meat and vegetables. A warm and comforting family favourite!

Serves 6

Ingredients:

  • 400g dried pasta
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3 large chicken breasts - cut into bitesize chunks
  • 1 large onion - chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper - chopped
  • 1 yellow bell pepper - chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic - peeled and minced
  • 1 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 800g chopped tomatoes
  • 120ml double cream
  • 100g fresh baby spinach
  • 6 rashers of bacon - cooked
  • 100g strong cheddar cheese - grated
  • 100g mozzarella - grated
  • a pinch of salt and pepper

To serve

  • a small bunch of parsley

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C.
  2. Boil a large pan of water and cook the pasta for 1 minute less than recommended on the pack, then drain.
  3. Whilst the pasta is cooking, heat the oil on a medium heat, in a large frying pan. Add the chicken, and cook until just sealed - about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the onion and cook for a further 3-4 minutes until the onion is softened.
  5. Add the chopped peppers, salt, pepper, garlic, tomato puree, oregano and thyme. Stir and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Stir in the tinned tomatoes and cream, bring to a gentle bubble, then add the pasta, spinach and cooked bacon. Stir everything together, then transfer to a large baking dish.
  7. Top with the cheddar and mozzarella, and place in the oven for 20-25 minutes, until the cheese is golden brown.
  8. Take out of the oven and top with parsley before serving.
